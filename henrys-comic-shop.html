Questions?

How does the subscription work? ▼
It's simple! Fill out the sign-up form with your email address, then Venmo $20 to @HenrysComicShop. Once Henry sees your payment, he'll start sending you 1 new comic every week — straight to your inbox!
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes! Just email Henry at henryscomicshop@gmail.com and he'll stop your subscription. Easy as that — no questions asked!
What kind of comics does Henry make? ▼
Henry draws all kinds of comics — superhero adventures, funny animal stories, space explorations, and mystery adventures. Each week is a brand-new surprise!
How exactly does payment work? ▼
After signing up, Venmo $20 to @HenrysComicShop. That covers 4 comics for the month. At the start of each new month, send another $20 to keep your comics coming!
How will I receive my comic each week? ▼
Henry will email your comic directly to the email address you signed up with. Make sure to check your inbox (and spam folder, just in case!) every week for a new delivery.
